Who We Are

KreatorKart is a platform designed to bridge the gap between emerging brands and talented influencers. Born out of the Indian Institute of Management, Rohtak as a web development project, our mission is to make influencer-brand collaborations simple, transparent, and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

To connect startups with the right influencers — and influencers with the right opportunities — through a structured, user-friendly, and transparent platform.

We believe that every brand, regardless of size, deserves access to authentic influencer partnerships that drive real results.

Our Story

The idea for KreatorKart came from a common challenge: early-stage startups struggle to find creators within budget, while micro and mid-tier influencers struggle to get discovered.

Our team set out to solve this by building a space where influencers can showcase their work, clearly state their rates, and receive serious, organized collaboration requests. For brands, KreatorKart offers an easy-to-use discovery tool that filters influencers based on category, budget, and audience size — eliminating the guesswork from influencer marketing.
